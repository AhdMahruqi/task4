PCR Calculator

The PCR Calculator is a tool designed to assist researchers and scientists in performing Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) as a way to help our customers from doing manual calculations!, a fundamental technique in molecular biology used to amplify specific DNA sequences. The form collects input parameters essential for setting up a PCR reaction, including the amount of template DNA, the concentration of primers, and the total reaction volume.

These calculations are based on common PCR protocols used in labs worldwide where a certain percentage of the reaction volume is allocated for the primer and template DNA to be added in the PCR machine we provide!.
